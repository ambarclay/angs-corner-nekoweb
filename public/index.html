>>welcome to my website

(best viewed in firefox on a computer)

hello there everyone, i'm ang! thanks for stopping over to my little corner of the internet. this is just a place for me to put basically anything i want, think of it like all of my social media except it's one page that i coded on my own :D

my goal in creating this website was to make something nostalgic- something that reminded me of my own days on the early internet, and to get away from social media. i wanted to get that late 90s/ early 2000s vibe, apply my own aesthetic and interests, and hopefully fart out something good. and hey look, this is what i ended up with! not too bad if i do say so myself.

take a look around what i've got here, but be warned- i'm a complete coding newbie and this website is just for fun, so it's not very elaborate and there's probably mistakes everywhere (not to mention the code itself is a complete mess). i'm learning as i go and i hope to keep improving this page as i cram more info into my little brain.
