why am i here?

why am i here?

so, i'm a 90s kid, if you couldn't tell by the design aesthetic of my site, and i really love the old internet. the one problem: i was born a little too late to experience the wonders of the old internet for myself. i saw some geocities and angelfire sites (shout out ted the caver), but by time my internet use was in full swing things like personal websites had all but died out, and sites like facebook were the cool new thing.

hot take (maybe not so hot): i think social media has been the downfall of overall creativity on the internet. not to mention engineered to be completely addicting. and boring. you ever notice how every popular website looks pretty much the same? pretty lame if you ask me.

i learned about the indie web not too long ago, and it really sparked something in me. i just had to make a website in that old web 1.0 style that i used to see all the time when i was younger. the interent now is so boring, so i thought i should do my part in making it interesting again.

reject modernity, embrace tradition, as they say.

honestly, i'm just kinda sick and tired of being put into boxes (and being constaly tracked and served ads) on social media. i think its almost impossible to fully express yourself on places like facebook, instagram, and twitter. you can only control who you follow and what you post and nothing else, but with a website, everything is made by you, for you.

if i want a page to talk about my interests, i can do that. write some blogs? sure! put all of the images i've collected over my time on the internet? hell yeah! make pages obsessing over things no one else i know irl cares about? absolutely! there's just so much more that i can do here than i can do anywhere else.

plus, people who care about personal websites are pretty cool :D

so yeah, this place is pretty much just my creative outlet. somewhere for me to be nostagic for things that were just out of my reach. a place where i can forget about the horrors of existing and pretend like it's 2000 again without a care in the world. and if other people come here and like what i've put out there, then i think i've accomplished something good.
